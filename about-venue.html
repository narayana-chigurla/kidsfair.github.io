About Venue

Hyderabad International Trade Expositions Limited, popularly called as HITEX, was established in 2003 by State Government under public-private partnership with Ace Urban Developers Private Limited with a vision to create a world class venue for organizing and hosting trade fairs and events. Over the years, HITEX has been a host to 1500+ national and international trade fairs, events and exhibitions and has organized its own events which contributed to the development of trade and commerce in the State.

In a very short time, HITEX has carved a niche for itself as an integrated facility for events, exhibitions, conferences, live shows and expositions. Centrally located in India and ideally located at the heart of Hyderabad, we are a premier MICE destination. With over a decade of rich and colorful history, we have emerged to be ‘VENUE FOR SUCCESS’.

Since its inception, HITEX is dedicated to embracing, achieving, maintaining and improving of standards and best practices.
